Copyright & Permissions

Acknowledging the purpose, prayer, and stewardship behind this work.

Real Pentecost: More Than a Moment, A Life in the Spirit is an original work written and maintained by Bradley Hobbs.

Every article, chapter, devotional, reflection, and study throughout this website has been prayerfully developed through personal Bible study, meditation upon God's Word, seasons of worship, supplication, and the experiences of everyday life.

The thoughts presented here are not intended to diminish sincere believers or elevate personal opinion, but are written from a sincere love for Jesus Christ, His church, and the enduring truth revealed through Scripture.

Like every disciple, the author has experienced moments of joy, seasons of uncertainty, answered prayers, disappointments, restoration, and the faithfulness of God through life's storms. Those experiences have shaped the reflections found throughout these pages while continually pointing back to the authority of God's Word.

It is the prayer of the author that every reader will look beyond the written words and encounter the living Christ through the Scriptures, growing in faith, hope, love, and steadfast devotion to the apostles' doctrine.
