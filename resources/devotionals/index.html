"And they continued steadfastly in the apostles' doctrine and fellowship, and in breaking of bread, and in prayers."

Why These Devotions?

These reflections were written as companions to Real Pentecost. Each one returns to Scripture, encouraging readers to look beyond tradition and rediscover the living testimony of Jesus Christ through the apostles' doctrine and the continuing work of the Holy Ghost.

Pentecost was never intended to become a distant memory. The same promise that transformed ordinary men and women continues to call believers into repentance, worship, faithful discipleship, and steadfast devotion to God's Word.

May these brief meditations encourage a deeper love for Scripture and a renewed desire to walk daily in the life of the Spirit.
