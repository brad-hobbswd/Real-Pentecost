A Continuing Journey

Growth in Jesus Christ is not measured by the number of pages we read but by the faithfulness with which we continue in His Word. Every article, devotional, and Bible study on this site has been written with a single desire: to encourage believers to return to Scripture with open hearts and renewed expectation.

The apostles continued steadfastly in doctrine, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayers. That same invitation remains for every generation. Scripture continues to reveal Jesus Christ, strengthen faith, and transform ordinary lives through the power of the Holy Ghost.

Whether you are beginning your journey or have walked with the Lord for many years, my prayer is that these resources will encourage a deeper love for the Word of God and a greater desire to follow the biblical pattern revealed throughout the book of Acts.
