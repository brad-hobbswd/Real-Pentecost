Why These Resources?

Every study and devotion on this page is designed to encourage a thoughtful return to Scripture. Rather than relying upon tradition or opinion, these resources invite readers to rediscover the apostles' doctrine and the continuing work of Jesus Christ revealed throughout the Word of God.

From repentance and baptism in the name of Jesus Christ to the gift of the Holy Ghost, prayer, fellowship, and holy living, each study builds upon the biblical pattern established by the early church and faithfully preserved through Scripture.

Whether you are beginning your journey or continuing your study, may these resources strengthen your faith, deepen your understanding, and encourage a steadfast walk with Jesus Christ.
