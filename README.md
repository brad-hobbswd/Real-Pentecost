# Real Pentecost
**More Than a Moment, A Life in the Spirit**
Real Pentecost is a Scripture-centered website and companion book that invites readers to rediscover the biblical pattern revealed through the life, death, resurrection, and promise of Jesus Christ.
Rather than presenting Pentecost as a historical event remembered only through tradition, this project explores the continuing life of repentance, the saving name of Jesus Christ, the gift of the Holy Ghost, the apostles' doctrine, fellowship, prayer, holiness, and faithful witness.

## ✨ Project Philosophy
Every page is designed around one central conviction:
> Pentecost was never intended to remain a moment remembered only through history.
The promise poured out in Jerusalem became the beginning of a people transformed by repentance, the saving name of Jesus Christ, the gift of the Holy Ghost, steadfast devotion to the apostles' doctrine, fellowship, prayer, holiness, and faithful witness.
