Welcome

Pentecost changed more than a single day in Jerusalem. The promise of Jesus created a people who continue steadfastly in truth, prayer, fellowship, and faithful witness. Real Pentecost invites readers to rediscover that biblical pattern through Scripture, thoughtful study, and the continuing life of the Spirit.
