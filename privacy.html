Privacy Policy

Respecting every visitor with honesty and transparency.

Real Pentecost is a static website hosted through GitHub Pages and is intended for reading, reflection, and personal Bible study.

This website does not intentionally collect personal information, create user accounts, or require registration.

If you voluntarily contact the author through an email address provided on this site, your message will be used solely for correspondence and will not be sold or shared with third parties.

External websites linked throughout Real Pentecost maintain their own privacy practices and policies.

As the ministry grows, this policy may be updated to reflect additional resources or services while continuing to respect the privacy of every visitor.
