About the Author

A teacher, a father, and a lifelong student of God's Word.

Bradley Hobbs is an educator whose passion for learning has always been accompanied by a deep love for Scripture. Whether in the classroom or through personal study, he believes that truth has the power to shape lives, strengthen faith, and inspire meaningful transformation.

As a father, his greatest desire is to leave a legacy rooted not in accomplishments but in a sincere love for Jesus Christ and His Word. That conviction became the foundation for Real Pentecost, a study that invites readers to return to the biblical narrative with fresh eyes and an open heart.

Rather than presenting Pentecost as a historical event preserved only in memory, Bradley encourages readers to discover the continuing life revealed through repentance, the name of Jesus Christ, the gift of the Holy Ghost, the apostles' doctrine, fellowship, prayer, holiness, and faithful witness.

His prayer is that every reader will encounter Jesus Christ through the Scriptures and experience the enduring promise first proclaimed on the Day of Pentecost.
